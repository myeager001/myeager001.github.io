The players each have three styles of pieces listed and can move 3 strikers, 1 keeper, and one pegger

The keeper can move and interact with the ball in all the same fashion a striker can with the added bonus of moving in the keepers box.
  2. Strikers: The strkers move around the board and control the movement of the ball. The striker has three actions per turn.
